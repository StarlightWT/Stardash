How to play

The goal of the game is to get as close to the number 21 without going over, while getting a higher total than the dealer.

You can either "stand" or "hit", if you "stand" your total is locked in and it's the dealer's turn. The dealer draws a card if their total is <17. If the dealer's total is higher than yours, without the dealer going over 21. You lose. If your total is higher than the Dealer's without going past 21 you win.

If you get 21 in your first 2 cards it's called a "BlackJack" which means you win 1.5x your bet. Otherwise winning results in winning double your bet.
